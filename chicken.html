Chicken Curry

Chicken curry is a versatile Indian dish where tender chicken is simmered in a richly spiced gravy, with regional variations bringing unique flavors to every recipe.

Ingredients:

  • 500g chicken breast, diced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp ginger, grated
  • 2 tbsp curry powder
  • 400ml coconut milk
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add the chopped onion, minced garlic, and grated ginger, and sauté until softened.
  2. Add the diced chicken to the pan and cook until browned on all sides.
  3. Stir in the curry powder and cook for another minute to release the flavors.
  4. Pour in the coconut milk, season with salt and pepper, and let it simmer for 20-25 minutes until the chicken is cooked through and the sauce has thickened.
  5. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves if desired and serve hot with rice or naan bread.

Enjoy your meal!
