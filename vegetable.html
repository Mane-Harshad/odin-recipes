Vegetable Stir Fry

Vegetable stir fry is a quick, versatile, and nutritious dish featuring crisp-tender vegetables cooked in a flavorful sweet and savory sauce, ready in as little as 10–30 minutes.

Ingredients:

  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 broccoli head, cut into florets
  • 2 carrots, julienned
  • 100g snap peas
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp grated ginger
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat the sesame oil in a large wok or pan over medium-high heat. Add the minced garlic and grated ginger, and sauté for about 30 seconds until fragrant.
  2. Add the sliced bell peppers, broccoli florets, julienned carrots, and snap peas to the pan. Stir-fry for 5-7 minutes until the vegetables are tender-crisp.
  3. Pour in the soy sauce and toss to coat the vegetables evenly. Cook for another 2 minutes.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Remove from heat and serve hot with steamed rice or noodles.

Enjoy your meal!
